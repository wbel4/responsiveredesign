Usability Problems

Chosen Webpage:

I chose this webpage as it is one I commonly visit and the UI/UX is noticeably bad for being a “hack news” site.

Usability Problems:

  • The website does not take up the entire screen on the Desktop version, not using screen real estate as well as possible.
  • The Desktop version also seems to have a lot of whitespace on the right side of the listings in conjunction with a very small font size which isn't very readable.
  • The navbar seems crammed and doesn't seem the most clickable. It also leaves the screen when scrolling down.
  • The login button is easy to miss as it is on its own little island in the navbar.
  • The gray text does not contrast enough with the off-white background and a lot of the text is too small to read.
  • A clearer division between listings would also help visually separate articles.
